Refund & Dispute Policy

Last updated: March 2026

All donations on Nammerha are held in escrow and only released upon GPS-verified delivery of materials. Your funds are protected at every step.

1. How Escrow Protection Works

When you donate to a reconstruction project, your funds are not immediately transferred to the supplier or contractor. Instead, they are held in a secure escrow account managed by the Platform.

Funds are only released when: (a) the assigned engineer submits GPS-verified spatial proof of material delivery, (b) photographic evidence is reviewed and approved, and (c) the homeowner confirms receipt. This triple-verification ensures your donation reaches its intended purpose.

2. Refund Eligibility

Pending Donations

Full refund available before escrow release. Cancel anytime from your donor portal.

Released Donations

Once funds are released after verified delivery, refunds are not available. Disputes must be filed within 14 days.

Stalled Projects

If a project shows no progress for 90+ days, donors may request a full refund from escrow.

3. Dispute Resolution

We take disputes seriously and provide a structured resolution process:

1

Submit dispute via your Donor Portal or email support@nammerha.com

2

Our team reviews spatial proof evidence within 4 hours

3

Independent auditor verification if evidence is contested

4

Resolution issued within 7 business days

4. Processing Times

Type Timeframe
Pending donation cancellation Instant
Approved refund processing 5–10 days
Dispute resolution 7 days
Stalled project refund 14 days

5. Anti-Fraud Commitment

Nammerha employs GPS-timestamped spatial proofs, image hash verification, SDN/sanctions screening, and real-time escrow monitoring to prevent fraudulent activity. Any evidence of manipulation results in immediate account suspension and escalation to relevant authorities.

6. Contact for Refund Requests

For refund inquiries, contact us at support@nammerha.com or visit our Contact Page.

Your donations are protected by escrow from the moment of payment until GPS-verified delivery is confirmed.
