02 — القصة

دبوس إعادة الإعمار — The Reconstruction Pin

Three layers of meaning encoded in a single, memorable mark. Each element is semantically justified.

١

📍 دبوس الموقع — GPS Pin

The outer shape is a GPS location pin — the platform pinpoints exact reconstruction sites with spatial precision.

٢

🏠 البيت — The House

Inside the pin, the Ha (هـ) becomes a house with roof and window. "Nammerha" means "We rebuild IT" — the "it" is the home.

٣

💨 المدخنة والدخان — Chimney & Smoke

The Alif (ا) rises as a chimney. Smoke wisps = life returning, warmth, human activity restored.

٤

ن — حرف النون — The Nun Letter

The pin silhouette itself echoes the letter Nun (ن) — the first letter of "نعمّرها" — creating a typographic anchor.
